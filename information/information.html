Right To Education

The right to education ensures that every individual has access to quality learning opportunities without discrimination, empowering them to develop their potential and contribute to society's progress. It promotes equal opportunities, fosters personal growth, and builds a foundation for a more equitable and knowledgeable world

Stop Child Labour

Childhood is for learning, playing, and growing, not for laboring. Let's ensure every child's right to a safe and nurturing environment, free from exploitation."

Stop Child Abuse

Every child deserves love, protection, and respect. Report any signs of abuse immediately to authorities for swift intervention and justice

Right To Health

Children have the inherent right to access healthcare services and enjoy optimal health, ensuring their physical, mental, and emotional well-being as they develop and thrive

Right To Survival

The right to survival for children encompasses access to basic necessities like food, shelter, healthcare, and protection from harm, ensuring their well-being and development. It's a fundamental human right crucial for fostering a healthy and dignified childhood

Right To Protection

Children have the inherent right to be safeguarded from harm, exploitation, and abuse, ensuring their physical, mental, and emotional well-being, as well as their rights to education, healthcare, and a nurturing environment.

Right To Identity

The right to identity for children ensures their recognition as individuals with inherent dignity, encompassing their name, nationality, and family ties, fostering their sense of belonging and protection under international law

NO Child Marriage

Child marriage is a violation of human rights, denying children their childhood and potential. Legal measures and community education are crucial to eradicate this harmful practice.

Right Against Discrimination

Every child deserves love and care, irrespective of differences, with equal opportunities fair and square.
